Tulip Pot

Elegant yellow tulips standing tall in a simple pot, perfect for spring.Planting tulips in pots involves using a quality potting mix in a container with drainage holes,
planting the bulbs pointy-end up, and providing them with eight to twelve weeks of cold for winter dormancy. After the cold period, move the pots to a sunny location and water them regularly.
